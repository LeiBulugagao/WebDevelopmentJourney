My Ginger Cat, Chinchin


How he looks like

He is a short-haired ginger cat with a warm orange coat and faint tabby stripes running along his face and body. His fur looks smooth and well-kept, with lighter cream tones around his muzzle and inner ears. His eyes are large and round, appearing light green, with one eye slightly turned outward. His ears are upright and pointed, with a soft pink tone inside. He has a small, triangular nose and long white whiskers that fan out from his cheeks. His face is gently rounded, giving him a soft, calm look. He’s wearing a light-colored shirt with yellow trim and simple printed patterns, which fits snugly around his body.


What Chinchin likes to do

Chinchin is a male cat with beautiful orange fur. His favorite person in the whole world is my mom. He follows her everywhere to the kitchen, sofa, even to the bathroom. He loves to eat chicken and hotdog the most. When my mom cooks these, Chinchin runs and meows loudly until she gives him a little piece. Most of the time, he is very sleepy. He can nap anywhere, on the bed or on a chair. And he is super clingy. He always wants to be near someone. If you sit down, he will jump on to your lap. If you walk away, he will follow you and rub against your legs. Chinchin is a sweet, lazy, loving cat who makes everyday warmer.
