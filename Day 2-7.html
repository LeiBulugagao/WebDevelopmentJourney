Lei's Favorite Food Categories

- Filipino dishes are known for their rich flavors and comforting meals. Some of my favorite Filipino foods are adobo and tinola because they are delicious and popular in many homes in the Philippines.

- Fast food is easy to prepare and loved by many people around the world. Pizza is one of my favorite fast foods because of its cheesy taste and different toppings.

- Desserts are sweet foods usually eaten after meals or during snacks. Ice cream is my favorite dessert because it is cold, sweet, and refreshing, especially during hot days.

- Pasta dishes are flavorful and filling meals that come in many styles. Pesto pasta is one of my favorites because of its creamy texture and tasty basil sauce.
